About Knock Lock

We rigged a car door to listen for a secret knock and when
the pattern matches the door will unlock.

Built for convenience

A lifesaving, alternative key for those who lock themselves out of their cars like myself

How It Works

The Arduino hack detects car knocks and the time intervals between each knock to determine the pattern
